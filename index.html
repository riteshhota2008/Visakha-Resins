Our Work

Services

     

  • Long Oil Alkyds

    • Long oil alkyds are characterized by having oil content greater than 55%. They are used extensively for interior and exterior architectural finishes, deck coatings, and chalk adhesion modifiers for latex paint. The high oil content helps give a smooth brushing action. These resins are infinitely soluble in aliphatic type solvents. Some high solids systems may be cut in aromatic or oxygenated solvent systems.

  • Medium Oil Alkyds

    • Medium oil alkyds are typically modified with 40-55% oil content. They are faster drying than the long oil alkyds. Typical solvent systems for these products are the aliphatic solvents. Some may be cut in aromatics or oxygenated solvents for high solids. Most will accept infinite dilution with aliphatic solvents.

  • Short Oil Alkyds

    • Short oil alkyds are characterized by having less than 40% oil content. These types of resins are usually cut in aromatic or oxygenated solvents and have very limited compatibility with aliphatic solvents. Short oil alkyds are almost exclusively used for industrial finishes. Most short oil alkyds can be crosslinked with melamine or urea resins via the alkyd hydroxyl functionality.
